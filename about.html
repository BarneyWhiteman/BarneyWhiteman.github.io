About Me

Who am I?

I'm a Software Engineer based in Brisbane, Australia. I enjoy creating games, desktop apps and creating visual interactions.

I started coding when I was 12 years old by making (terrible) flash games, during high school I started learning Python and HTML/CSS and continued to make games. I continued my programming journey by studying a Bachelor of Software Engineering (Hons) at the University of Queensland, from which I graduated in 2019. After doing a few internships during my studies and finishing my degree, I now work full time as a Software Engineer. I spend a lot of my free time tinkering with Processing, trying to come up with interesting game mechanics and interactive code.

There are a number of other activities I enjoy as well, I play guitar and build things. I also play a lot of social sport and I like to keep fit by cycling.
