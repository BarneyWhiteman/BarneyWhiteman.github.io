About Me

Who am I?

I'm a 20 year old fourth (and final) year Software Engineering student at the University of Queensland in Brisbane Australia. I enjoy creating games and playing with neural networks, I love to experiment with code and learn!

I started coding when I was 12 years old by making (terrible) flash games, during high school I started learning Python and HTML/CSS and continued to make games. When I'm not busy with assignments, I spend a lot of time tinkering with Processing trying to come up with interesting game mechanics and interactive code.

There are a number of other activities I enjoy as well, I play guitar and a lot of social sport and I like to keep fit by cycling.
